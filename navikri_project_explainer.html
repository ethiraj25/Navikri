Job Description Creation & Requirements Gathering
70%
Automated
What the SLM does
  • Generates a fresher-friendly JD template — broad skill requirements, flexible experience criteria, emphasis on learning ability and academic background.
  • Pulls non-negotiable requirements from three departments: Deploy Team, HR, and Project Lead. These are hard gates — failing any one = auto-elimination.
  • Converts all requirements into a structured scoring rubric with weighted criteria.
  • Publishes JD to job portals and manages inbound applications.
SLM: JD Generator SLM: Rubric Builder SLM: Portal Publish
Non-Negotiables collected from
  • Deploy Team — Technical must-haves (e.g. a specific language, OS knowledge, toolchain familiarity).
  • HR — Compliance requirements (degree completions, work authorization, joining date flexibility).
  • Project Lead — Domain-specific minimums (e.g. awareness of a particular methodology, coursework alignment).
⊘ Non-Negotiable Gate 👤 HR final JD approval
Human Review

HR reviews and approves the generated JD, non-negotiable criteria, and scoring rubric before it goes live. Can edit any section.

Fully Automated · SLM-Driven
Resume Screening, Scoring & Non-Negotiable Elimination
92%
Automated
What the SLM does
  • Parses every incoming resume — extracts skills, academic history, projects, certifications, and experience.
  • Checks each resume against the non-negotiable list. Any candidate missing even one non-negotiable is automatically eliminated — no human review at this step.
  • For all surviving candidates, scores the resume against the JD rubric and assigns a Resume Points Score (RPS) out of 100.
  • Flags academic standouts, strong project portfolios, or notable certificates for easy HR spotting.
SLM: Resume Parser ⊘ Non-Neg Elimination ◈ Resume Points Score
Scoring Breakdown (RPS)
  • Skills match vs JD requirements — highest weight.
  • Academic background — degree, CGPA, institution tier (fresher-weighted heavily).
  • Projects / Internships — relevant work, even personal projects count.
  • Certifications & courses — bonus points for aligned learning.
  • Soft indicators — leadership roles, clubs, publications.
◈ Points out of 100 👤 Edge case review
Human Review

HR is notified of any ambiguous or borderline non-negotiable cases (e.g. incomplete info) before final elimination is confirmed.

What the SLM presents
  • Presents all scored candidates as a ranked, easy-to-read visual dashboard — sorted by Resume Points Score.

  • Reject candidates who don't meet HR's judgment even if they passed the SLM gate.

Automated · SLM-Driven
MCQ Test Preparation, Approval & Secure Dispatch
85%
Automated
What the SLM does
  • Generates a tailored MCQ question paper based on JD requirements — sectioned by domain knowledge, aptitude, verbal reasoning, and role-specific technical questions.
  • Submits question paper to HR / Project Lead for approval before dispatch — no paper goes out without sign-off.
  • Upon approval, sends automated test invitations to all approved candidates with venue details: Innovacx, Hyderabad, date, time, and secure network instructions.
  • Manages invitations, reminders, and confirmations automatically.
SLM: Paper Generator 👤 Paper approval SLM: Auto-Invite
Test Logistics
  • Venue: Innovacx, Hyderabad — secured, proctored environment.
  • Network: Isolated, secured intranet — SLM serves the test on a controlled interface.
  • Sections: Technical / Domain · Aptitude · Verbal · Role-Specific.
  • Candidates cannot access the test paper outside the venue network.
  • SLM records start time, end time, and submission for every candidate.
SLM: Secure Delivery SLM: Submission Tracker
Human Review

HR and Project Lead must approve the question paper before it is dispatched to candidates. No test goes live without this gate.

Fully Automated · SLM-Graded
MCQ Auto-Grading & Test Points Score
95%
Automated
What the SLM does
  • Auto-grades every submitted MCQ paper immediately upon completion.
  • Computes a Test Points Score (TPS) per candidate — overall score plus a section-wise breakdown (Technical, Aptitude, Verbal, Role-Specific).
  • Ranks all candidates by TPS and flags section-specific standouts (e.g. candidate scored low overall but excelled in verbal or technical sections).
  • Presents ranked results to HR with section breakdowns for each candidate.
SLM: Auto Grader ◈ Test Points Score SLM: Section Breakdown
Bookmarking at this stage
  • HR reviews the test results dashboard.
  • Can bookmark any candidate for a specific section performance — e.g. "low overall but strong verbal" or "high technical, worth tracking."
  • Bookmarks carry a label/reason set by HR and are attached to the candidate's profile for all subsequent phases.
  • HR then makes the final call: who advances to Phase 6 and who is eliminated.
⭐ Section Bookmark 👤 HR decides who advances
Human Review

HR reviews ranked test results, applies bookmarks as needed, and confirms the final list of candidates advancing to Phase 6.

Automated · Post Human Approval
Automated Call Letter Generation & Dispatch
90%
Automated
What the SLM does
  • For every HR-approved candidate, SLM auto-generates a personalised call letter — includes candidate name, role, reporting date, venue, documents to carry, and contact details.
  • Dispatches call letters via automated email with a confirmation receipt mechanism.
  • Tracks delivery — notifies HR of any undelivered emails.
  • Sends a reminder email 24 hours before the joining/next-step date.
SLM: Letter Generator SLM: Auto Email Dispatch SLM: Delivery Tracker
Human controls
  • HR reviews and approves the call letter template before the first batch goes out.
  • HR can add a personal note or attachment (e.g. onboarding document) to the template.
  • Dispatch does not begin until HR gives final go-ahead.
  • HR receives a dispatch report — who was emailed, when, and delivery status.
👤 Template approval 👤 Final dispatch go-ahead SLM: Dispatch Report
Human Review

HR approves the call letter template and gives the final go-ahead for dispatch. SLM handles the rest — sending, tracking, and reminders.
